## 2. WHAT NOT TO SAY

These 5 lines will kill trust with food business owners. Never use them:

1. **"I hope this message finds you well"** — Instant delete energy. They know it's a template.
2. **"I came across your profile and was impressed"** — You didn't. You Googled them. Be real.
3. **"I would love to collaborate with you"** — Collaborate? You're selling a service. Say what it is.
4. **"We offer end-to-end digital marketing solutions"** — Jargon. They run a dhaba, not a boardroom. Speak human.
5. **"Let me know if you're interested"** — Weak. Always end with a specific, low-pressure CTA like "Want me to send a free sample week?"
